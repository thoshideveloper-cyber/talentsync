UX Designer — Product Design
Company: DesignMatter Studio
Location: Bengaluru (Remote-first)
Salary: ₹15–22 LPA

About the Role
We are hiring a UX Designer to own the design system and drive user experience across our B2B SaaS product. You will work closely with product and engineering to ship thoughtful, accessible interfaces.

Responsibilities
- Lead end-to-end UX for new features — research, wireframes, prototypes, handoff
- Maintain and evolve the design system in Figma
- Run usability tests and synthesise feedback
- Advocate for accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA compliance)

Requirements
- 3–5 years of UX/product design experience
- Proficient in Figma; comfortable with interaction prototyping
- Experience conducting user research and usability testing
- Portfolio demonstrating shipped B2B or SaaS product work

Nice to Have
- Experience designing for data-heavy dashboards
- Basic front-end skills (HTML/CSS) for better developer collaboration

Compensation & Benefits
- Salary: ₹15–22 LPA
- Fully remote with quarterly team offsites
- Health insurance (employee + dependants)
- 25 days paid leave
- Annual design tools & conference budget
