Data Analyst — Growth & Insights
Company: TalentSync Technologies
Location: Mumbai (Remote-friendly)
Salary: ₹12–18 LPA

About the Role
Join our analytics team to turn raw data into business decisions. You will own dashboards, run ad-hoc analyses, and partner with marketing, product, and finance.

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain dashboards in Metabase and Tableau
- Write clean, efficient SQL queries against our data warehouse
- Identify trends and deliver weekly insight reports to leadership
- Partner with engineers to instrument new product features

Requirements
- 2–4 years of experience in a data analyst or BI role
- Proficient in SQL and Python (pandas, matplotlib)
- Experience with at least one BI tool (Tableau, Metabase, Looker)
- Strong communication skills — able to explain data to non-technical stakeholders

Nice to Have
- Experience with dbt or Airflow
- Familiarity with A/B testing methodology

Compensation & Benefits

- Health insurance (employee + spouse + children)
- 20 days paid leave
