AI Product Engineer – GenAI Platform
Company: Nexus AI Labs
Location: Bangalore (Hybrid – 3 days on-site)

About the Role

production APIs — and move with the speed and hunger of a true tech ninja.

- 2–4 years of experience with Python, FastAPI, and REST APIs
- Hands-on with at least one LLM provider (OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic)
- Experience with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, or pgvector)
- Strong understanding of RAG pipelines and prompt engineering techniques
- Familiarity with LangChain or LlamaIndex

Good to Have

- Experience with A/B testing frameworks for model evaluation
- Published papers or open-source contributions in the AI/ML space

Responsibilities

- Collaborate with product and design to ship GenAI features to end users

- Lead internal tech-talks and drive the AI-first engineering culture
