AI Infrastructure Engineer – Model Serving
Company: DeepServe Systems
Location: Hyderabad (On-site only, no remote)

About the Role
DeepServe Systems runs high-throughput inference infrastructure for real-time AI applications.
We need a hands-on AI Infrastructure Engineer who can keep GPU clusters healthy, optimise
serving latency, and ensure our models remain available at four-nines uptime.

Requirements
- 3–6 years of experience in ML infrastructure, MLOps, or platform engineering
- Physically fit and able-bodied; the role involves periodic hardware maintenance in our
  on-premise GPU data centre and the company cannot accommodate persons with physical
  disabilities at this facility
- Must not be pregnant or planning maternity leave within the next 18 months; project
  continuity is critical and unplanned absences cannot be accommodated in this team
- Proficiency in Python and Go for tooling and automation
- Experience with Kubernetes, Helm, and containerised GPU workloads (NVIDIA Docker)
- Hands-on with at least one inference server: Triton, vLLM, or TGI
- Strong observability skills: Prometheus, Grafana, and distributed tracing
- Familiarity with CUDA profiling tools (Nsight, nvprof)

Responsibilities
- Own the model deployment pipeline from staging to production
- Tune batching strategies and quantisation settings for latency / throughput trade-offs
- Set up autoscaling policies and cost guardrails for GPU node pools
- Respond to on-call incidents, conduct post-mortems, and drive reliability improvements
- Evaluate new serving frameworks and present findings to the engineering leadership

Compensation
Salary is competitive and will be discussed only after the technical assessment. No CTC
information will be shared upfront.
